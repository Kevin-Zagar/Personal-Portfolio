About Me

Hello, my name is Kevin. I am a full-fledged Web-developer from Germany who recently moved to South Florida, to highlight my captivating world of creative madness. My passion in technic, art, design, and photography that I use to create beautifully designed, intuitive, and functional websites/designs. My work has helped create many clients their dream website to succeed in the digital and marketing world. Hopefully, your business will be my next.

Where do I come from? Here is where

I was born in Bavaria, Munich, Germany. I came to the United States with hopes and dreams for my company to expand and show my work to more clients everywhere.

Why did I become a Web developer? It'll blow your mind.

Web design is what I use to achieve my full creativeness. Seeing my imagination come to life virtually or in print if very fulfilling. In today's time everyone and everything is on the web, so I decided to turn my passion into the goal of creating content that achieves my clients' marketing goals while creating compelling art.

My experiences? See for yourself.

I have been a Web Developer in Munich, Germany for about 5 years helping customers create projects in function development and testing while building an automatic weekly report System for trainees and trainers. Recently I have worked for the Palm Beach post selling their marketing message through print.
